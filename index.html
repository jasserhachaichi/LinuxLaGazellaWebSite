What is La_Gazella ?

is an online Linux operating system website coded with HTML, JavaScript, CSS, AJAX and PHP. La_Gazella aims to be the ultimate reference for users wishing to explore, learn and master Linux. By providing detailed information, an interactive platform and complementary resources, the site strives to facilitate the adoption of Linux and allow users to take full advantage of its advanced features.
